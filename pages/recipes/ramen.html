Tik Tok Ramen

I found this recipe on a Youtube Short and decided to try it... now I'm obsesed! It takes 10 minutes and it's so much better than regular old ramen.

Ingredientes

  • Shin ramen packet
  • Kewpie mayo
  • Egg yoke
  • Garnish: scallions, fried egg, chili oil

Steps

  1. Pour seasoning packet, egg yold, and kewpie mayo into a bowl and mix
  2. Cook noodles and veggie packet according to package instructions
  3. Once boiling, add a little water to the seasoning mixture
  4. Once noodles are cooked, add everything to the seasoning mixture and enjoy!
