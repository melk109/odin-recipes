Grandma's ravs

Description

Grandma's ravs is a family favorite! The day after Thanksgiving we get together to make more than 500 raviolis for Christmas. The process isn't easy, but the results are always worth it!

Remember to double the recipe if you want to get 500 or so.

Ingredients

Fresh pasta dough

  • 4 large eggs
  • 4 cups flour
  • Pinch of salt
  • Drizzle of olive oil

Filling

  • 1 lb veal or ground chicken
  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 packages chopped frozen spinach (1.2 cups)
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 12 slices Genoa Volpi salami (.25 lb)
  • 6 slices of prosciutto (.25 lb)
  • 2 slices of American bread, no crust, soaked in milk
  • 1 tsp nutmeg
  • Basil to taste
  • Parsely to taste
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare filling
  2. Pour flour on smooth board
  3. Make a hold in center and put eggs, salt, and oil in the middle
  4. Knead for 10 minutes
  5. Let rest for 15-30 minutes, covered
  6. Run small pieces of dough through mixer, startin at 1, until desired thickness
  7. Fill raviolis, cut apart, and freeze
