Pat's pizza

Description

This is my favorite pizza! Pat and I made it up together, but he does most of the work. If you want it to be even better, top it off with fermented garlic honey.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough from Whole Foods (or homemade)
  • Small can tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Prosciutto
  • 1/2 Large red onion
  • Olive oil
  • Banana peppers, optional

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450F
  2. Divide and roll out dough
  3. Brush the outer edges with olive oil
  4. Brush on tomato sauce in center
  5. Add cheese and toppings
  6. Cook pizza for about 13 minutes
